How It Works

From conversation to
perfect match in seconds

No forms, no endless filters. Just describe what you want and let the AI do the rest.

1
💬
Tell the AI what you want
Type naturally in plain language — "I want a remote data analyst role in Singapore, $80k minimum." Or use slash commands: /role, /location, /salary for instant filtering without a full sentence.
2
🤖
AI finds, scores, and checks every job
Tulifo searches 50+ boards at once, calculates a match score for each result, and runs a five-point trust check — freshness, source, scam signals, company credibility, and salary transparency.
3
🚀
Apply with full confidence
Every card shows a match score, trust rating, AI-written pros and cons, salary info, and a direct apply link. Save jobs, compare them, and move forward knowing exactly what you're applying to.
